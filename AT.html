The Appalachain Trail

About This Project

This minimalist inforgraphic was created with the intent of providing basic information on hiking the Appalachain Trail, especially for potential thru-hikers. My fascination with the Appalachain Trail comes from its unique combination of nature and history. While the trail's natural wonders are amazing, it also has a human history and a legacy that comes with a thru-hike. With this graphic, I attempted to capture both of those things by answering questions like: What do hikers experience on the tail? What type of people hike the trail? Data was processed in ArcMap and Illustrator was used for vector design, type, and icons.
