About Me

  • I am a third year undergraduate student majoring in geography and environmental studies at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. I am primarily interested in the intersection of human geography and environmental geography. In my professional life, I hope to work in an environmental career, specifically one that targets climate change. I am fascinated by cities and their potential role in mitigating and adapting to a changing global climate.
  • As a cartographer, I believe that my design skills allow me to communicate my ideas in a unique way. The use of maps has increasingly become an important part of social media and can spread information effectively, in an impactful and appealing way. I hope to supplement my education at the University of Wisconsin-Madison with these essential geography skills.
